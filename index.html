About Me

Developer & Designer

Hello! I'm Shadreck Mubanga, a web developer and graphic designer with a passion for developing visually appealing and functional digital experiences. With a background in both coding and design, I bring to each project a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic flair.

My interest in web development and graphic design began with a curiosity about how technology and art might work together to create meaningful solutions. I've honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and numerous design tools over the years to transform ideas into appealing websites, user interfaces, and branding materials.

In addition to my professional work, I'm an active contributor on GitHub, where I've collaborated on diverse projects within the open-source community. This experience has allowed me to sharpen my coding skills, learn from others, and give back to the developer community.

For the past two years, I've also had the privilege of working as a freelancer on Fiverr, where I've had the opportunity to work with clients from around the world. This experience has taught me the importance of effective communication, meeting deadlines, and tailoring my skills to meet the unique needs of each project.

Please explore my portfolio and don't hesitate to contact me. I'm excited to bring your ideas to life!
